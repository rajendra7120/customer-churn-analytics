# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis

## Overview

This project analyzes customer churn for a telecommunications company using Python, SQL, and Tableau.

The objective is to identify the key factors influencing customer churn and provide actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention.

## Key Insights

- Month-to-month customers show the highest churn.
- Fiber optic customers are more likely to churn.
- Electronic check users have a higher churn rate.
- Customers with shorter tenure are at greater risk of leaving.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Promote longer-term contracts.
- Encourage automatic payment methods.
- Launch retention campaigns during the first six months.
- Target high-risk customer segments with personalized offers.
